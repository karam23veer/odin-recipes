Delicious Pizza recipe

Description

fully loaded with cheddar cheese and veggies the pizza is the most liked snack around the world.

Recipe

  • Prepare a dough with wheat and yeast powder and store it for 30 minutes.
  • knead the dough and make a flat base.
  • take some cheese and spread evenly on it
  • put pizza sauce and other toppings on it.
  • bake in a oven for 10 minutes and serve hot.
